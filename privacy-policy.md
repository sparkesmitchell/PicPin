# PicPins Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 20, 2026

## Overview
PicPins is a photo annotation app that allows users to take photos and add pins with notes to them.

## Data Collection
PicPins does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers. All photos, pins, and notes created within the app are stored locally on your device only.

## Camera and Photo Library Access
The app requires access to your device's camera and photo library solely to enable core app functionality. Photos are never uploaded or shared without your explicit action.
